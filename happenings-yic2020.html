Qualifiers for Young Inventors Challenge 2020

June 15, 2020 at Malaysia (Virtual event)

Seven out of eight entries from PSHS-CRC qualified to participate in the YIC SPECIAL EDITION 2020, which will be conducted through a submission of video-based inventions by the participating teams. Each competing team will submit an Invention Pitch Video, along with a Project Report on their invention via email.
