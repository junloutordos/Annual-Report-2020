International Invention and Innovative Competition 2020 (INIIC 2020) - Malaysia

April 16 - 20, 2020 at Penang, Malaysia (Virtual)

Chaperoned by Engr. Patrick D. Cerna, PhD, SST IV and Engr. Jecelyn R. Elorta, SST III, selected PSHS-CRC scholars participated in the International Invention and Innovative Competition 2020 (INIIC 2020) last April 16-18, 2020, an event seeking innovative, cost-effective, and practical solutions to accessibility-related barriers in global communities. INIIC 2020 provided the participants with the exposure to the latest sources of research and cutting-edge innovation in research to achieve world-class education. The competition also introduced ideas and innovations of the latest technologies which can be used in the teaching and learning process environments. PSHS-CRC had two (2) entries namely PATAK and MASISTEMA which were facilitated through virtual presentation and participation. The two teams from the PSHS-CRC won the Gold and the Bronze awards, respectively.
