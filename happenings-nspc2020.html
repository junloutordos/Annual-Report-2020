National School Press Conference 2020

March 9-13, 2019 I Tuguegarao City, Cagayan,

In cognizance to the implementation of Republic Act 7079, otherwise known as Campus Journalism Act of 1991, where enhancement trainings of School Paper Advisers and campus journalist is one of the areas to consider for an effective, efficient, and pro-active campus papering, the Department of Education (DepEd) through the Tuguegarao City Division conducted the 2020 National Schools Press Conference (NSPC) on March 9-13, 2020 with the theme "Empowering Communities through Campus Journalism". The Philippine Science High School Caraga Region Campus, as part of the delegation of Caraga Region, in its continued compliance to all statutory and regulatory requirements participated to the said event and bagged the several national level awards. PSHS CRC scholars competed for individual and group competitions on various fonns of journalistic writing and broadcast reporting and came out victorious with the following awards:
